Finley's Farm Shop

About us

A local part of the community

Nestled in the heart of the community, Finley's Farm Shop has been a cherished local gem since 1851. Steeped in tradition, our family-run shop is more than just a place to buy fresh, locally sourced produce—it's a gathering spot for friends, neighbours, and visitors alike. Over the generations, we’ve remained committed to supporting local farmers and artisans, ensuring that everything we offer reflects the very best of our countryside.

Our mission

What we stand for

At Finley’s Farm Shop, our mission is rooted in sustainability, organic farming, and ethical practices. We believe that nurturing the land with care and respect is essential for producing the highest quality goods while protecting the environment for future generations. From growing produce free from harmful chemicals to sourcing ethically farmed and cruelty-free products, we’re dedicated to making choices that align with our core values.

Core Values

Caring about our land

At our farm, our team picks each vegetable at its peak, ensuring the freshest flavours and highest quality for our customers. From crisp lettuces to vibrant carrots, every item on our shelves reflects the dedication and hard work that goes into cultivating produce the way nature intended. It’s this commitment to freshness and sustainability that sets Finley’s apart.
